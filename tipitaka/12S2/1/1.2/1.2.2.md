## Summary

Moá¸·iyaphaggunasutta discusses the four nutriments for the sustenance of beings, as per [1.2.1Â Ä€hÄrasutta](1.2.1.md). The sutta explains that these nutriments are causes for the arising of various phenomena, which ultimately lead to dukkha. The BhagavÄ emphasises that it is not suitable to ask who consumes these nutriments, but rather what is the cause for these nutriments to be required. The sutta concludes by describing the cessation of the causes ultimately leading to the cessation of dukkha.
