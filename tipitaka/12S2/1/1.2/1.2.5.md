## Commentary

This is an amazingly insightful sutta, with the Buddha explaining that the concept of sammÄdiá¹­á¹­hi ("right view") is avoiding the extremes of regarding everything we perceive as being "real", or "not real", but understanding that our perceived world is constructed and dependent on underlying causes (dependent origination). With the cessation of these causes, dukkha is eliminated from our perceived world. Note that the Buddha is neither implying dukkha is "real" or "not real", but it is constructed, and by ceasing construction, it is no longer perceivable, and therefore cease to exist in our perceived world.
